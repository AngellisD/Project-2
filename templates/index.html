"Traffic Collisions in LA">

The Los Angeles area has long been synonymous with bad traffic. Much effort has been expended to reduce the problem, but as often-cited research from economist Anthony Downs found, traffic on urban freeways always increases to meet maximum capacity. A 2009 study called "The Fundamental Law of Road Congestion" reiterated these findings with recent data from U.S. cities. Given the number of cars in the Los Angeles area, there will be many traffic collisions. This project will detail the particularly accident-prone/ cities in the Los Angeles area.
